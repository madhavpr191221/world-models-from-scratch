Method

What happens here

  1. Take unlabeled STL-10 images.
  2. Train a vision encoder with VICReg.
  3. Freeze the encoder.
  4. Extract features from the labeled split.
  5. Fit a simple linear classifier on top of those features.

Why this test

Why the probe is useful

  • If the features are useful, a simple classifier should work.
  • If the features are weak, the classifier cannot recover much.
  • Keeping the encoder frozen makes the test easier to read.
